Academic Offerings

What is the STEM strand?

The STEM strand offers a wide range of career opportunities for graduates. With the increasing demand for professionals in STEM fields, students who graduate from this program have excellent prospects for finding well-paying jobs. Some of the career opportunities that are available for STEM graduates include:

  • Engineer - Engineers design and develop new products, processes, and systems. There are many different types of engineers, including mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, civil engineers, and aerospace engineers.
  • Scientist - Scientists conduct research to discover new knowledge about the world around us. There are many different types of scientists, including biologists, chemists, and physicists.
  • Data Analyst - Data analysts analyze and interpret large sets of data to identify patterns and trends. They work in a variety of fields, including marketing, finance, and healthcare.
  • Architect - Architects design buildings and structures. They work in a variety of settings, including residential, commercial, and industrial.
  • Medical professional - Medical professionals work in a variety of fields, including healthcare, biotechnology, and pharmaceuticals. They include doctors, nurses, and researchers.

Overall, the STEM strand offers students an excellent opportunity to pursue a career in fields that are in high demand. Graduates of this program have a wide range of career opportunities available to them, and they are well-equipped to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

HUMSS Strand

In the HUMSS strand, you will explore the complexities of the human experience, from our history and culture to our political and social structures. You will develop critical thinking skills and learn to analyze and interpret complex ideas and texts. These skills are in high demand in today's fast-paced world, where the ability to think critically and communicate effectively is essential for success.

But the HUMSS strand is much more than just an academic program. It offers a wide range of career opportunities, from journalism and law to psychology and social work. You could work in government, non-profit organizations, or the private sector. The possibilities are endless.

At the heart of the HUMSS strand is a commitment to social justice and community engagement. You will learn about the issues facing our society today, and be challenged to think critically about how we can create a more just and equitable world. You will have the opportunity to engage with your community and make a real difference in the world.

So why choose the HUMSS strand? Because it offers the perfect blend of practical skills, intellectual growth, and social engagement. It prepares you for a wide range of careers, while also providing a rich and fulfilling educational experience. It is the perfect choice for anyone who wants to make a difference in the world and achieve their full potential.

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand

If you're looking for a strand that can provide you with the necessary skills to succeed in the fast-paced world of business and finance, then the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand is the perfect choice for you.

In the ABM Strand, you'll learn about the principles of accounting, finance, marketing, and management. You'll develop skills in data analysis, problem-solving, and decision-making. You'll also learn about entrepreneurship and how to start and run your own business.

But the ABM Strand isn't just about theoretical knowledge. You'll also have the opportunity to gain practical experience through internships, case studies, and simulations. This hands-on experience will give you a competitive edge in the job market and help you stand out from the crowd.

With the ABM Strand, you'll have the skills and knowledge to succeed in any of these fields. You'll also be prepared to pursue higher education, such as a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or Accountancy.

So why choose the ABM Strand? Because it offers a solid foundation in business and finance, practical experience, and a wide range of career opportunities. It's the perfect choice for anyone who wants to succeed in the world of business.

The TVL Track: Your Gateway to a Thriving Career

Looking for a career that's both challenging and rewarding? Consider the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track, which offers a wide range of practical skills and hands-on experience that can help you succeed in today's fast-paced world.

In the TVL track, you will gain valuable skills in a variety of fields, including hospitality and tourism, automotive technology, electrical installation and maintenance, and more. You will learn from experienced professionals in the industry, and have the opportunity to apply your knowledge through real-world projects and internships.

But the TVL track is much more than just a vocational program. It also offers a strong academic foundation that prepares you for further education and career advancement. You will develop critical thinking skills and learn to work effectively in teams, as well as gain a deep understanding of the social and cultural context of your chosen field.

Whether you're just starting out in your career or looking to make a change, the TVL track is an excellent choice that can help you achieve your goals. So why wait? Enroll now and take the first step toward a brighter future!

Data Privacy Policy

Introduction

This document outlines the data privacy policy for the Multi-information Academic Portal for Students or MAPS of San Jose Litex Senior High School. MAPS is a web-based platform that allows students to access various academic and administrative services, such as enrollment, grades, schedule, announcements and advisories, module resources, and career services.

Personal and sensitive personal information

MAPS collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal and sensitive personal information of students, such as name, student number, email address, academic records, and other information relevant to the provision of the services. The student portal also uses cookies and other technologies to enhance the functionality and security of the platform.

Data privacy compliance

MAPS processes personal and sensitive personal information in accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA) and its implementing rules and regulations (IRR), as well as the policies and guidelines of San Jose Litex Senior High School and the University of the Philippines System. MAPS respects the rights of data subjects to access, correct, object, erase, or withdraw consent to the processing of their personal and sensitive personal information, subject to certain limitations and conditions.

Data security and access control

MAPS implements reasonable and appropriate physical, organizational, and technical measures to protect personal and sensitive personal information from unauthorized access, use, modification, disclosure, or destruction. MAPS also ensures that only authorized personnel have access to personal and sensitive personal information on a need-to-know basis.

Data sharing and transfers

MAPS may also transfer personal and sensitive personal information to other jurisdictions, subject to compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Changes to the policy

MAPS may update this data privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the legal or regulatory environment, or in the operations or services of the platform. MAPS will notify students of any material changes through email or other means of communication.

Consent and responsibility

By accessing or using MAPS, students agree to this data privacy policy and consent to the processing of their personal and sensitive personal information as described herein. Students are responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential and for logging out of the platform after each session. Students are also encouraged to review this data privacy policy periodically and to contact us if they have any questions or concerns.
