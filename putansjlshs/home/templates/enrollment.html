Student Enrollment

Enrollment Details

Junior high-school graduates are automatically qualified for enrollment at San Jose-Litex Senior High School. Students who are qualified must follow the enrollment guidelines and compile the necessary requirements for enrollment. Enrollees are required to submit the following:
  • Original and photocopy of the report card (Form 138)
  • Original and photocopy of the birth certificate
  • Two (2) recent 2x2 ID pictures
  • Accomplished enrolment form

Enrollment Requirements

Enrollment Procedure

Visit the Admission Page Compile the necessary requirements Go to the prescribed enrollment dates for your grade level Get the proof of enrollment Wait for the confirmation of your enrollment

Visit the Admission Page

Go to the San Jose-Litex Senior High School website and navigate to the Admission page. Read the instructions and guidelines for enrollment carefully. Ensure that you meet the eligibility requirements before proceeding with the enrollment process. If you have any questions or concerns, you may contact the Admissions Office through the provided contact information on the website.

Compilation of Documents

Compile all the necessary documents for enrollment. These include an original and photocopy of your report card (Form 138), an original and photocopy of your birth certificate, two recent 2x2 ID pictures, and the accomplished enrollment form. Ensure that all the documents are complete, accurate, and up-to-date. If there are any missing or incomplete documents, you will not be allowed to proceed with the enrollment process.

Enrollment Period

During the enrollment period, proceed to the San Jose-Litex Senior High School campus and submit all the necessary documents to the Admissions Office. Pay the required fees and charges, and attend any orientation sessions, if there are any. Ensure that you follow all the guidelines and instructions provided by the school during the enrollment process.

Get Proof of Enrollment

After submitting all the necessary documents and paying the required fees, you will receive a proof of enrollment from the Admissions Office. This may be in the form of a receipt or an official document. Keep this proof of enrollment in a safe and secure place, as you will need it for future transactions with the school.

Wait for Enrollment Confirmation

After completing the enrollment process, wait for confirmation from the school that you have been officially enrolled. The school will inform you of the date of the start of classes and provide you with any other information that you need to know. If you have any questions or concerns, you may contact the school’s Admissions Office for assistance.

Data Privacy Policy

Introduction

This document outlines the data privacy policy for the Multi-information Academic Portal for Students or MAPS of San Jose Litex Senior High School. MAPS is a web-based platform that allows students to access various academic and administrative services, such as enrollment, grades, schedule, announcements and advisories, module resources, and career services.

Personal and sensitive personal information

MAPS collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal and sensitive personal information of students, such as name, student number, email address, academic records, and other information relevant to the provision of the services. The student portal also uses cookies and other technologies to enhance the functionality and security of the platform.

Data privacy compliance

MAPS processes personal and sensitive personal information in accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA) and its implementing rules and regulations (IRR), as well as the policies and guidelines of San Jose Litex Senior High School and the University of the Philippines System. MAPS respects the rights of data subjects to access, correct, object, erase, or withdraw consent to the processing of their personal and sensitive personal information, subject to certain limitations and conditions.

Data security and access control

MAPS implements reasonable and appropriate physical, organizational, and technical measures to protect personal and sensitive personal information from unauthorized access, use, modification, disclosure, or destruction. MAPS also ensures that only authorized personnel have access to personal and sensitive personal information on a need-to-know basis.

Data sharing and transfers

MAPS may also transfer personal and sensitive personal information to other jurisdictions, subject to compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Changes to the policy

MAPS may update this data privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the legal or regulatory environment, or in the operations or services of the platform. MAPS will notify students of any material changes through email or other means of communication.

Consent and responsibility

By accessing or using MAPS, students agree to this data privacy policy and consent to the processing of their personal and sensitive personal information as described herein. Students are responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential and for logging out of the platform after each session. Students are also encouraged to review this data privacy policy periodically and to contact us if they have any questions or concerns.
